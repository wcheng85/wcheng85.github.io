Uluru "Ayers Rock"

Uluru or Ayers Rock is a large rock formation located in the Northern Territory in central Australia and was discovered on July 19, 1873 by William Goose. Uluru stands approximately 1,142 feet high, 2.2 miles long and 1.2 miles wide. Uluru is listed as a UNESCO World Heritage Site and is sacred to the Aboriginal people living of the area.
