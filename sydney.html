Sydney

Sydney Opera House

Located in Sydney Harbour, the Sydney Opera House is one of the most famous tourist attractions in Sydney. The Sydney Opera House was designed by Danish architect Jorn Utzon and was opened on October 20, 1973. The architectural design features a modern expressionist design, with a series of large precast concrete shaped in form of shells. The Sydney Opera House hosts a number of performance venues such as a concert hall (the home of the Sydney Symphony Orchestra), multiple theatres, a playhouse, recording studio, and more.
