Uluru or Ayers Rock

Getting there: Fly to Ayers Rock Airport at Yulara or by car via the Northern Territory's Red Centre Way.

Places to stay: Stay at the Ayers Rock Resort or at the Ayers Rock Campground which are both located approximately 10 minutes away from the park.

Things to do:

  • Spend a few hours hanging outside the park and dining under the stars via the award-winning Sounds of Silence experience; or
  • Experience dining under the stars inside the park via the exclusive AAT Kings barbeque. Find out more by visiting aatkings.com

To learn more about visiting Uluru: visit Parks Australia's website.
